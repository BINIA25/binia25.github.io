Conference Agenda & Speakers

Day 1 - October 27
Day 2 - October 28

Day 1: October 27, 2025

09:00 - 10:30

Opening Session

Welcome remarks from CRPP, Jima University, Jima Municipality, Ministry of Peace

Keynote Address: Why Ethiopia Needs a Grand Narrative – Lessons from History and Global Practice

11:00 - 13:00

Session 1 – Historical and Comparative Context

Ethiopia's political settlements and the narratives that shaped them

Comparative experiences from South Africa, Tunisia, Northern Ireland, and Colombia

Panel discussion: National identity, shared memory, and political legitimacy

14:00 - 15:30

Session 2 – Mapping the Need and Risks

Risks of fragmented national discourse without a unifying narrative

Potential political, social, and economic dividends of an agreed narrative

Stakeholder perspectives: governance, security, cultural identity, development policy

Day 2: October 28, 2025

09:00 - 10:30

Session 3 – Enabling Frameworks

Policy and institutional reforms to support future narrative development

Structural arrangements for inter-ministerial and multi-sector coordination

Intellectual stewardship: role of academia, historical scholarship, and cultural production

11:00 - 12:30

Session 4 – Building Momentum and Partnerships

Identifying key actors and their mandates

Resource mobilization and capacity-building strategies

Proposed sequencing toward institutionalization of the Grand Narrative

14:00 - 16:00

Session 5 – Synthesis and Adoption of the Jima Consensus

Presentation and discussion of the draft Jima Consensus Statement

Endorsement of the Framework Roadmap and Partnership and Process Map

16:30 - 17:30

Closing Session

Reflections from senior leaders and commitment to follow-up actions

Media briefing and public release of the conference outcomes
