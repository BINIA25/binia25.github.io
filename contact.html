Conference Partners

Provides national leadership for the Conference, with responsibility for coordination of national media engagement.

Jima University

Serves as the academic host institution, offering the Conference venue and technical support.

Oromia Regional State

Provides strategic oversight and supports resource mobilization for the Conference.

Jima City Municipality

Leads local government coordination, including logistics and accommodation facilitation.
