About the Conference

Ethiopia stands at a critical juncture in its history, where the need for a unifying national narrative has never been more pressing.

Our contemporary political discourse is shaped by deep historical legacies, contested narratives of nationhood, and diverging visions for the future. This fragmentation poses significant challenges to national cohesion, development, and democratic consolidation.

This National Conference offers a platform for constructive dialogue among Ethiopia's diverse stakeholders to outline frameworks for institutionalizing a Grand Narrative that recognizes our rich diversity while establishing shared national purpose.

Conference Details

Venue

Jima University Conference Hall, Jima Town, Oromia Regional State, Ethiopia. The venue offers state-of-the-art facilities for plenary sessions and breakout discussions.

Format

Two-day conference featuring keynote addresses, panel discussions, working groups, and plenary sessions. Hybrid participation available for international attendees.

Languages

Simultaneous translation in Amharic, Oromiffa, English, and other Ethiopian languages will be provided to ensure inclusive participation.

Outcomes

The conference will produce a policy framework document and roadmap for institutionalizing Ethiopia's Grand Narrative through education, media, and cultural institutions.

Conference Partners

This national initiative is made possible through the collaboration of key institutions committed to Ethiopia's unity and development.
